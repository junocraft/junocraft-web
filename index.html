  • Information

    How we begun

    We formed from the League of Nations (LoN), and it was a project that was started in 2016 as a fun experimental game in which players split into factions and fight each other using plugin guns.
    The League quickly became popular and received pivotal additions such as a world-map, list of laws (charters), and a hierarchy.
    In late 2017 the League of Nations unofficially disbanded and multiple attempts to bring it back to life have been made.
    Fortunately, with the launch of JunoCraft, the League of Nations is coming back for good and hopefully is never reset again.
    We hope to bring back all the fun memories that original League of Nations created and have an even greater time.
